Memes is the number one place to express your ideas and connect with a diverse community of cultures, ages, and beliefs. Our ultimate goal is to empower the world through a unified language – in memes.

We created these Community Guidelines so you can help us protect the Memes community. By using Memes, you agree to these guidelines and our Terms of Service. We’re wholly committed to maintaining a safe and friendly environment for all members in our community and use these guidelines to adhere to this goal. Dismissing these rules or acting contrary to the terms set forth herein may result in your account being deleted or disabled, content removed from the platform, or other restrictions and limitations placed on your account.

  • Share content that you’ve personally taken or have the appropriate permissions to share. You may be liable for infringing upon intellectual property
  • Share content that is appropriate for all audiences.
  • Pornography, hate speech, trolling, terrorism or harassment will not be tolerated.
  • Sexualizing of any persons under the age of 18 will result in immediate bans and your information submitted to the proper authorities.
  • Do not share graphic images for sadistic pleasure or to glorify violence.
  • Foster and provide meaningful interactions. Spam or abuse of the system will not be tolerated.
  • Do not engage in any artificial or ingenuine strategies to collect likes or followers.
  • Do not repeatedly contact people for commercial purposes without their consent.
  • Ensure that your content and actions within the community comply with all applicable laws.

We are first and foremost, a community. For this community to thrive, we must foster a positive welcoming experience for all people, collectively, regardless of their shapes, sizes, beliefs, or differences. We, without hesitation, remove content that contains credible threats or hate speech, content that targets private individuals to degrade or shame them, personal information meant to blackmail or harass someone, and repeated unwanted messages.

We have zero tolerance regarding violence or attacks towards any member of our community based on their race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religious affiliation, disabilities, or diseases. We may, however, allow for controversial subject matter to be shared in an effort to dispute or drive awareness towards an issue. Our policy and ethics committee have the sole discretion to remove your content should they determine that it violates our community guidelines.

Members of the community may report any and all activity they believe to be violating our community guidelines. Such reports may include threats or danger to public and personal safety, physical harm, theft, vandalism, or other harm. We thoroughly review all investigate all reports by our community.

What you can do as a user:

  • Each of us is an integral part of our growing community. Your participation and help in keeping our community clean and thriving is what will help everyone enjoy their experience with us. If you find something that you think may violate our guidelines, please help us by reporting the piece of content. We have a policy and ethics committee that reviews all reports to remove content that doesn’t meet our guidelines.
  • Should you find content that you disagree with, or a user that you dislike, yet that user has not violated any of our community terms, you can choose to unfollow or block that person using our built-in tools. If someone leaves you a comment that you do not want to be seen by others, you also have the option to delete that comment as an administrator.
  • Should a member of our community violate law, we may work with law enforcement.
